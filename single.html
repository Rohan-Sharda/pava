OUR VISION

About 1/3 people of India needs glasses but are unable to access them due to various reasons. Spycraft aims to decrease the number of such people marginally in coming years.

OUR MISSION

As a company that began as a way of doing something new in this industry, providing affordable eyewear to the masses without compromising the quality is our most important motive. Moreover, we have started the 'SightUp' movement wherein we donate 1 percent of our profits for the treatment of blind patients in rural areas where it is not accessible.

We have already helped 1M+ people for their eye treatment because of our amazing customer base.

Great Quality
